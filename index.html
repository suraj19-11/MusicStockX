KYC Process

PAN Verification

✓ Step 3

Add your Indian bank account. We do a ₹1 penny-drop to verify ownership in real-time.

Royalty calculation formula

Transparent and verifiable. Every rupee accounted for.

Trust & Compliance

Built for fintech compliance

Production-grade security, double-entry accounting, and full regulatory compliance from day one.

🔐

Full KYC + PAN

PAN verification, face match, and bank penny-drop. Fully compliant with RBI PPI norms and PMLA guidelines.

📒

Double-entry ledger

Every rupee movement creates two auditable rows. Wallet balances are always reconcilable to the paisa. All amounts stored in paise — no floating point.

⛓️

Polygon blockchain

ERC-1155 NFTs on Polygon PoS. Ownership is on-chain and publicly verifiable. 20-block confirmation before mint is finalised.

🔄

Idempotent payouts

Every payout carries a unique idempotency key. Double-claim and duplicate royalty crediting is impossible by design.

📸

Immutable snapshots

Ownership snapshots on the 1st are write-once. No row can ever be updated or deleted — a permanent, tamper-proof audit trail.

🏦

Razorpay + RazorpayX

Regulated payment rails for collection and payout. IMPS/NEFT transfers only to KYC-verified Indian bank accounts.

Regulatory Compliance
✓

RBI PPI Norms
